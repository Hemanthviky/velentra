What is Velentra?

Velentra is a business management platform that helps companies manage smart employee ID, visitor management, gate operations, and HR workflows from one place.

What is Smart-ID?

Smart-ID helps employees access digital ID, attendance, leave requests, approvals, notice board, pulse surveys, and expense claims through a mobile-first experience.

What is Smart-Gate?

Smart-Gate helps organizations manage visitors, QR passes, OTP verification, host approvals, gate entry, exit tracking, and visitor reports.

What is Smart-People?

Smart-People is an HR management platform for employee records, attendance, leave, payroll, productivity analytics, reports, and performance workflows.

Can Velentra be customized?

Yes, Velentra can be customized based on company workflows, departments, approval levels, and reporting needs.

Is Velentra suitable for multiple industries?

Yes. Velentra supports corporate offices, manufacturing, education, healthcare, logistics, construction sites, and commercial buildings.
