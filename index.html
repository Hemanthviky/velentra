Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Velantra.

Learn how the connected platform fits your teams, workflows, and industry.

Talk to our team
What is Velantra?

Velantra is a business management platform that helps companies manage smart employee ID, visitor management, gate operations, and HR workflows from one place.

What is Smart-ID?

Smart-ID helps employees access digital ID, attendance, leave requests, approvals, notice board, pulse surveys, and expense claims through a mobile-first experience.

What is Smart-Gate?

Smart-Gate helps organizations manage visitors, QR passes, OTP verification, host approvals, gate entry, exit tracking, and visitor reports.

What is Smart-People?

Smart-People is an HR management platform for employee records, attendance, leave, payroll, productivity analytics, reports, and performance workflows.

Can Velantra be customized?

Yes, Velantra can be customized based on company workflows, departments, approval levels, and reporting needs.

Is Velantra suitable for multiple industries?

Yes. Velantra supports corporate offices, manufacturing, education, healthcare, logistics, construction sites, and commercial buildings.
