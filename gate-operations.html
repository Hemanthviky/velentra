How it works

A Simple 6-Step Visitor Journey.

A clear, secure experience from the first scan to final check-out.

Scan QR Code

▤2

Register Information

Visitor enters contact, host, vehicle, and visit details.

✓3

OTP Verification

Email OTP verification ensures authenticity and security.

◎4

Approval Process

The host reviews and approves or rejects the request.

▧5
